Coach Matt Rhule's Impact on Nebraska Football

Matt Rhule - Head Coach

Matt Rhule is in his third season as Nebraska's head coach in 2025. Rhule led Nebraska to its first bowl appearance in eight years in 2024, guiding the Huskers to a victory in the Pinstripe Bowl.

🏆 2024 Season

Led Nebraska to 7-6 record and first bowl victory in 8 years

📈 Program Builder

12 years head coaching experience - Temple, Baylor, NFL Panthers

🎯 Proven Success

One of 12 active FBS coaches to guide 3 schools to bowl games

Coaching Honors & Awards

  • 🏆 2019 Sporting News National Coach of the Year
  • 🏆 2019 AFCA Region IV Coach of the Year
  • 🏆 2019 Big 12 Coach of the Year

The Rhule Method

Highly regarded as a program builder and player developer, Rhule led successful turnarounds at Temple and Baylor. His teams have increased or matched their win total from the previous season in each of his nine seasons as a college head coach.

"Building champions on and off the field through culture, development, and Nebraska values."

Career Highlights

Temple Turnaround

Led Temple to back-to-back 10-win seasons (2015-16) for first time in program history. Beat Penn State for first time in 73 years.

Baylor Resurrection

Took Baylor from 1-11 to 11-3 in three seasons. School-record 11 wins in 2019 with Big 12 Championship Game appearance.

Nebraska Revival

First bowl game in 8 years, highest ranking (No. 22) in 8 years, undefeated vs. non-conference foes in 2024.

Academic Excellence

Under Rhule's leadership, Nebraska posted its highest team GPA in history and had more Academic All-Big Ten selections than any other school in 2024.

Rhule emphasizes developing student-athletes who excel both on the field and in the classroom.

Playing & Coaching Background

Playing Career: Linebacker at Penn State (1994-1997), helped Nittany Lions to 41-8 record with Big Ten title and three bowl victories.

Education: Bachelor's degree in Political Science (Penn State), Master's degree in Educational Psychology (Buffalo).

Personal: Native of New York City, married to Julie with three children: Bryant, Vivienne, and Leona.
